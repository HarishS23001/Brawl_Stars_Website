Brawl Stars Beginner's Guide

What is Brawl Stars?

Brawl Stars is a fast-paced mobile game made by Supercell. You play as unique characters called Brawlers in 3v3 team battles and solo or duo battle royale matches. Each Brawler has their own attacks, Supers, and abilities. The goal changes depending on the mode, but teamwork and strategy always matter.

Basic Controls

  • Left Joystick: Move your Brawler around the map.
  • Right Joystick: Aim and attack enemies.
  • Tap Attack: Quick fire at the nearest target.
  • Super Button: Unleash your powerful Super attack when charged.
  • Gadgets & Star Powers: Special abilities you unlock as you progress.

Main Game Modes

  • Gem Grab: Collect and hold 10 gems to win.
  • Showdown: A battle royale where the last player/team wins.
  • Brawl Ball: Score 2 goals before the enemy team.
  • Heist: Defend your safe while attacking the enemy’s safe.
  • Hot Zone: Control areas on the map to earn points.
  • Bounty: Earn stars by defeating enemies. The team with the most stars wins.

Basic Tips

  • Don’t rush in alone — work with your team.
  • If you’re the gem carrier in Gem Grab, play safe.
  • Use walls and bushes to dodge and surprise enemies.
  • Learn enemy Brawlers so you know how to counter them.
  • Save your resources — don’t waste coins or gems on weak upgrades.
