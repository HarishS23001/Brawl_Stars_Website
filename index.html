DAILY TIP OF THE DAY!

In Gem Grab, always prioritise collecting 10 gems and falling back to a safe position when you have 10 or more. Do not push aggressively if you are the Gem carrier, and protect your Gem Carrier if you are on one of the two lanes (Right or Left)!
